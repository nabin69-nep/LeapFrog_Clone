THE STORY

A leader-centric experience that makes their work easier, faster, and more effective

In September 2017, Laudio began a journey aimed at enhancing frontline manager productivity at health systems, and retaining more staff in a burnout-ridden industry. Laudio immediately partnered with Leapfrog for the discovery, design and development of their MVP.

Six years later,Laudio is deployed across US health systems, has raised Series B funding, and been recognized for their innovation and potential to disrupt the healthcare market. And Leapfrog's partnership with Laudio has grown too, having started with with a discovery and design team of five, and then growing to be a 50-person team across app development, data engineering, AI, DevOps, design, and product management.

Today, Laudio helps frontline managers in health systems such as Nurse Managers, personalize and connect with their teams in meaningful ways that improve performance, retention, quality, and patient experience. With Leapfrog by its side, Laudio has crafted a leader-centric experience that makes their work easier, faster, and more effective. They've amplified productivity with simplified and automated workflows for frontline managers and their staff. And they've created panoramic visibility across frontline teams that give up to real-time insights on what decisions to make and actions to take.

We're proud to be a pivotal partner on Laudio's continued journey to disrupt healthcare by helping those that deliver care do their jobs better and be happier.

Leapfrog's contributions include:

  • MVP launched within three months.
  • Integrated Machine Learning models to analyze turnover risk, showcasing the company's technological innovation in workforce management.
  • Large application engineering teams spanning React and Node ecosystems, Python, and more.
  • Releasing new features at rapid and predictable cadences.
  • Data integration, engineering and automation across multiple health system software that provide panoramic and always-up-to-date views of a frontline manager and their staff.
  • A dedicated SRE team that focuses on infrastructure scaling and performance, security, reliability, and incident response
  • A team of data analysts that plays a vital role in developing and maintaining the core analytics model that drives the Laudio platform and providing invaluable support to the healthcare industry by supplying benchmark data and trend analysis
  • Multiple UI/UX designers and Product Owners that help validate new features ideas upfront, and then perfect them as they hit the roadmap.
  • Support for Laudio achieving AICPA certified SOC2 Type II compliance.
  • Security and privacy-first culture of development and operations.
